Mission

By providing quality veterinary, agricultural, horticultural, aqua products and professional
services at reasonable prices to farmers, fishermen, hobbyists and dealers to ensure continuous
satisfaction and a sense of pride to all customers, business partners, community, employees and
shareholders.

Vission

To help and empower the farming and fishing communities and the private sector to produce
enough, accessible and affordable food for every Filipino and a decent income for all.
